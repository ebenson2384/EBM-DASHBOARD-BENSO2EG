**Expert Source 1: Gallup State of the Global Workplace 2025**
- **Source:** Gallup Workplace Insights
- **URL:** https://www.gallup.com/workplace/insights.aspx
- **Key Finding:** Employee engagement (driven by communication) correlates with 21% higher profitability
- **Problem Recognition:** Poor communication cited as top cause of employee disengagement
- **Solution:** Regular manager check-ins, clear goal communication, feedback loops
- **Implementation:** Requires manager training, communication systems, measurement

**Expert Source 2: SHRM Employee Engagement Survey Research**
- **Source:** Society for Human Resource Management
- **URL:** https://www.shrm.org/topics-tools/research
- **Key Finding:** 72% of HR professionals identify communication as critical to employee performance
- **Best Practice:** Transparent communication from leadership, multiple communication channels
- **Success Factor:** Two-way communication (not just top-down)

**Expert Source 3: HBR - "It's Time to Streamline How We Communicate at Work" (August 2025)**
- **Authors:** Todd Rogers and Charles Dorison
- **URL:** https://hbr.org/2025/08/its-time-to-streamline-how-we-communicate-at-work
- **Key Insight:** Communication overload reduces performance - quality over quantity matters
- **Recommendation:** Strategic communication editing, clear messaging, reduced meeting time
- **Impact on Performance:** Streamlined communication increases productivity and reduces burnout

**Case Study: Federal Agencies with High FEVS Scores**
- **Context:** Top-performing federal agencies consistently score higher on communication questions
- **Communication Practices:** Regular town halls, transparent leadership, accessible supervisors
- **Performance Outcomes:** Higher employee retention, better public service delivery
- **Lesson Learned:** Communication investment pays off in measurable performance
