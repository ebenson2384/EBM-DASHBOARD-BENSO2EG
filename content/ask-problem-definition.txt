## Clear Problem Statement
Dealing with a lack of employee engagement and satisfaction is an issue that has been developed from a lack of communication within the workplace.

## Why This Problem Matters
Without an effective level of communication in the workplace, this could lead to a lack of employee satisfaction. This could lead to decreased productivity and happiness so steps must be taken to prevent this.

### Stakeholder Impact
 Excess meeting time reduces capacity for focused work and slows delivery.

### Strategic Alignment
 Managers: Encourage open communication between co-workers and upper management.
 Front-line employees: Begin communicating feelings about work structure and quality of life within the workplace.
 Senior leaders: Less of a responsibility than the managers but necessary for them to be on board and encourage workplace autonomy.
