# Milestone 1 — 1-Day Manager Workshop Agenda (Pilot)

Purpose: Equip managers with practical meeting design skills and coaching techniques to implement meeting norms.

Duration: 1 day (6 hours including breaks)
Participants: 8–12 managers (pilot group)
Facilitators: L&D / external facilitator + project lead

Agenda
- 09:00 – 09:15 | Welcome, goals, and pilot expectations
- 09:15 – 10:00 | Evidence review: baseline findings from survey and meeting audit
- 10:00 – 11:00 | Principles of meeting design: agendas, timeboxing, roles, decision artifacts
- 11:00 – 11:15 | Break
- 11:15 – 12:15 | Practical exercise: redesign a common recurring meeting (small groups)
- 12:15 – 13:00 | Lunch
- 13:00 – 14:00 | Coaching practice: giving and receiving feedback to change meeting norms
- 14:00 – 15:00 | Implementation planning: team-level experiments and metrics
- 15:00 – 15:15 | Break
- 15:15 – 16:00 | Measuring success and sustaining change: dashboards, rituals, and governance
- 16:00 – 16:30 | Commitments & next steps: manager action plans and accountability

Materials to prepare
- Sample audit results and survey highlights
- Meeting redesign templates and quick-start playbook
- Follow-up coaching schedule and optional 1:1 slots

Follow-up
- 2-week check-in session (60 minutes) for pilot managers
- Peer coaching pairs assigned post-workshop
- 4–6 week review with senior sponsor to present pilot metrics
